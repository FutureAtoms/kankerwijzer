# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a respectful, welcoming place for contributors, reviewers, and users. Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and personal attacks are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in reviews and discussions.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and process, not people.
- Be especially careful when discussing medical topics that may affect patients or caregivers.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Trolling, insults, or hostility
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainers' safety or moderation guidance

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the community or the safety goals of the project.
